## What happened

Ares Management completed a record $30 billion fundraising, signalling continued institutional confidence in the private credit market despite earlier concerns about sector stress and funding availability.

## Key figures

- **Fundraising total:** $30 billion (record for Ares)
- **Market context:** Addresses 'doomsday' sentiment previously circulating in private credit

## Why it matters for the lower middle-market

Robust capital formation at tier-one credit managers like Ares directly impacts deployment capacity and pricing dynamics for lower middle-market borrowers. Sustained fundraising eases concerns about credit availability and refinancing risk for sponsors and operators in the $10M–$100M revenue segment, who rely on diverse private credit sources for acquisition financing, working capital, and dividend recaps. This outcome suggests reduced pressure on covenant terms and pricing during the current credit cycle.
