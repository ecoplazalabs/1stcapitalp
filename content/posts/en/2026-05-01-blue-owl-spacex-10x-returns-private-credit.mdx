## What happened

Blue Owl Capital reported exceptional gains on its SpaceX investment, stating it has realised a 10-fold return on the position. The announcement coincided with SpaceX preparing for what the firm characterised as a record IPO later in 2026.

## Key figures

- **SpaceX investment multiple:** 10X return achieved by Blue Owl on its initial capital deployed
- **Timing:** Expected SpaceX IPO launch planned for later in 2026

## Why it matters for the lower middle-market

This outcome underscores the potential risk-adjusted returns available through private credit and growth equity exposure, a sector increasingly accessible to lower middle-market sponsors and institutional co-investors. While SpaceX represents a venture-scale outcome, the demonstrated capital appreciation illustrates how disciplined exposure to high-growth private companies can generate portfolio outperformance. For CFOs and PE professionals evaluating fund managers and co-investment opportunities, such track records signal robust deal sourcing and portfolio management capability—factors that influence allocation decisions in the private credit space where dry powder remains substantial.
