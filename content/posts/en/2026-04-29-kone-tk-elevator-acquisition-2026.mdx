## What happened

Kone, a Finnish elevator manufacturer, has agreed to acquire German competitor TK Elevator for $34.4 billion. The combination will create the world's largest elevator maker and represents one of Europe's largest takeovers in recent years.

## Key figures

- **Deal value**: $34.4 billion
- **Target**: TK Elevator (Germany-based)
- **Acquirer**: Kone (Finland-based)
- **Market position post-deal**: World's largest elevator maker

## Why it matters for the lower middle-market

This high-profile cross-border consolidation in industrial services underscores the ongoing consolidation trend in European mid-market sectors. For lower middle-market operators and their sponsors, the deal demonstrates how scale acquisition strategies continue to reshape competitive dynamics. Cross-border M&A mechanics—particularly in regulated, capital-intensive verticals—remain relevant for LMM investors evaluating sector roll-up opportunities and exit multiples in engineered systems and recurring-revenue service businesses.
