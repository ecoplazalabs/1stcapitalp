## Why it matters for the lower middle-market

For lower middle-market operators and sponsors, Treasury yield movements directly influence the cost of debt financing and working capital. Rising yields typically compress valuations and increase the cost of acquisition debt, affecting deal structures and leverage multiples in cross-border M&A. Concurrent geopolitical uncertainty can also tighten credit availability and increase risk premiums on non-treasury fixed income instruments, placing pressure on smaller borrowers reliant on commercial bank facilities.
